# Money Health Score – India 🇮🇳

## Problem
95% of Indians do not have a clear financial plan. Most people do not know whether their savings, expenses, and loans are healthy.

## Solution
Money Health Score is an AI-powered personal finance mentor that gives users a clear score (0–100) and simple, actionable advice in under 5 minutes.

## Features
- Easy data input (income, expenses, savings, loans)
- Transparent money health score
- AI-style advisory tips
- Beginner-friendly and explainable logic

## Tech Stack
- Python
- Streamlit

## How It Works
1. User enters basic financial details
2. Rule-based scoring engine evaluates money health
3. AI advisory layer converts signals into simple guidance

## Impact
- Replaces expensive financial advisors
- Saves time and money for retail users
- Scalable to millions of Indian users

## How to Run
pip install streamlit  
streamlit run app.py
## 🏠 How a Middle-Class Indian Family Can Save & Invest

Most middle-class families struggle not because they earn less, but because they don’t have a clear financial plan.  
This project is designed specifically to guide such families in a simple and practical way.

### 1️⃣ Save First, Spend Later
Instead of saving what is left after expenses, this system encourages saving a fixed portion of income first — even ₹2,000–₹5,000 per month makes a difference.

### 2️⃣ Simple Budgeting Rule
A practical version of the 50–30–20 rule is followed:
- 50–60% for needs (rent, food, electricity, education)
- 15–20% for wants (shopping, outings, subscriptions)
- 20–25% for savings and investments  
Even 10–15% savings is acceptable for low-income households.

### 3️⃣ Emergency Fund (Top Priority)
Before investing, families should build an emergency fund equal to at least 3 months of expenses.
This fund should be kept in a savings account or liquid fund for safety and quick access.

### 4️⃣ Start Small with SIP Investments
The safest way for middle-class families to invest is through Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) in mutual funds.
Starting with ₹1,000–₹2,000 per month is enough to begin long-term wealth creation.

### 5️⃣ Insurance Before Investment
The system emphasizes protection first:
- Health insurance for the entire family
- Term life insurance for earning members  
It avoids mixing insurance with investment products.

### 6️⃣ Debt Control
High-interest debts like credit cards and personal loans reduce financial stability.
The AI mentor advises clearing such debts before starting aggressive investments.

### 7️⃣ Step-by-Step Action Plan
Unlike traditional tools, this AI mentor does not just show problems.
It provides clear instructions on **how to reduce expenses, increase savings, manage loans, and invest safely**.

### 🎯 Outcome
By following these steps, a middle-class family can gradually move from financial stress to financial confidence without depending on expensive financial advisors.
