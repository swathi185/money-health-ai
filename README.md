💰 AI Money Health Score

A 5-minute AI-powered financial wellness checker designed for Indian middle-class families.
The app helps users understand their financial health and gives clear, actionable steps to improve saving, investing, and planning — without needing a financial advisor.

🚀 Problem Statement

95% of Indians do not have a structured financial plan.
Professional financial advisors are expensive and inaccessible to most middle-class families.

Goal:
Build an AI-powered personal finance mentor that makes financial planning simple, fast, and accessible.

💡 Our Solution

AI Money Health Score converts basic financial inputs into a clear financial health score (0–100) across 6 key dimensions, along with personalized improvement suggestions.

This tool acts as a foundation layer for advanced features like FIRE planning, tax optimization, and portfolio analysis.

🧠 Features
✅ Money Health Score (0–100)

Calculated using explainable, rule-based logic.

✅ 6 Financial Dimensions
Emergency Preparedness
Insurance Coverage
Investment Discipline
Debt Health
Tax Awareness
Retirement Readiness
✅ Instant, Dynamic Results
No submit button
Score updates automatically when inputs change
✅ Personalized Action Plan

Clear steps a middle-class family can realistically follow.

🧾 Inputs Required
Age
Monthly income & expenses
Emergency savings
Health & term insurance status
Monthly SIP / investments
Loan EMI
Tax-saving usage
Retirement planning status
📊 Output
Money Health Score (0–100)
Financial health category (Excellent / Good / Needs Attention)
Detailed diagnosis per dimension
Actionable suggestions to improve financial health
